How to play

  • You have 21 guesses to identify the mystery virus (~170 in the pool).
  • Search by common name (e.g. "Ebola virus") or ICTV binomial (e.g. "Orthoebolavirus zairense") — both work in the search bar.
  • Each guess shows a badge with the deepest shared taxon — click it to open Wikipedia.
  • The dendrogram above your guesses grows to reveal the taxonomy tree.
  • Switch taxonomy mode (5 / 8 / 15-rank) at any time; switching resets the current game.
  • On a correct guess, a micrograph (where available) and a side-by-side comparison of all three classification systems is shown.

Taxonomy follows NCBI/ICTV 2023–2024. Binomial species names per ICTV 2021+ mandate (latinized where known).
