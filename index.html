How to play

  • You have 21 guesses to identify the mystery virus (~111 in the pool).
  • Search by common name (e.g. "Ebola virus") or ICTV binomial (e.g. "Orthoebolavirus zairense") — both work in the search bar.
  • Each guess shows a color-coded badge — color encodes how close you are (red = no overlap → purple = correct). The taxon name is not revealed; use the tree.
  • The ? node in the tree tracks the mystery virus's position — it moves closer to the answer as you home in on the right branch.
  • Click any named node in the tree to open its Wikipedia page.
  • Switch between Classic (5-rank) and Full ICTV (15-rank) at any time; switching resets the game.
  • On a correct guess, a micrograph and a side-by-side comparison of both classification systems is shown.

Taxonomy follows NCBI/ICTV 2023–2024. Binomial species names per ICTV 2021+ mandate.
