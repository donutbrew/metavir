How to play

  • You have 21 guesses to identify the mystery virus.
  • Each guess badge shows the deepest taxon you share with the answer — click it to open Wikipedia.
  • The dendrogram grows to show how your guesses branch from the mystery virus.
  • Switch taxonomy mode (5 / 8 / 15-rank) at any time — it changes what ranks are shown in the dendrogram and badges. Switching resets the current game.
  • On a correct guess a micrograph is shown (where available) and a side-by-side comparison of all three classification systems is displayed.
  • Use Show hint for a one-line clue.

Taxonomy follows NCBI/ICTV 2023–2024 classification.
